# ACC Tools - Monetization Strategy

## Product Positioning

ACC Tools is positioned as a **premium productivity suite** for construction professionals working with Autodesk Construction Cloud. The product targets:

- **Primary:** Construction project managers and site supervisors
- **Secondary:** Architects, engineers, and consultants
- **Enterprise:** Large construction firms and GCs

## Pricing Tiers

**Price:** $0/month

**Purpose:** User acquisition, product trial, hobbyist use

**Limits:**
- 10 documents/month scraping
- 5 site inspections/month
- 1 user account
- 100 MB storage
- Community support only
- ACC Tools branding on reports

**Features:**
- ✅ Basic PDF scraping
- ✅ Simple inspection forms
- ✅ Photo capture (max 5 per inspection)
- ✅ Basic report generation
- ✅ ACC read-only integration

- ❌ No custom templates

- ❌ No API access

**Target Users:** Individual contractors, students, evaluators

## Pricing Strategy

### Value-Based Pricing

Pricing is based on **value delivered** rather than cost:

- **Time Saved:** Automated scraping saves 10+ hours/week
- **Error Reduction:** ML extraction reduces manual errors by 90%
- **Efficiency Gains:** Faster inspections = more projects handled

**ROI Calculation for Pro Tier:**
- Cost: $99/month
- Time saved: 10 hours/week × 4 weeks = 40 hours/month
- Consultant rate: $100/hour
- Value: 40 hours × $100 = $4,000/month
- **ROI: 40x**

### Competitive Pricing

**Competitors:**
- Procore: $375+/month
- PlanGrid: $39-99/user/month
- Fieldwire: $39-59/user/month

**ACC Tools Advantage:**
- Lower entry price ($29 vs $39+)
- More generous free tier
- Specialized for ACC integration
- ML-powered extraction (unique)

### Psychological Pricing

- **$29** (not $30) - feels significantly cheaper
- **$99** (not $100) - under the $100 threshold
- **Annual discount** - 20% savings encourages commitment

## Monetization Tactics

### 1. Freemium Conversion

**Goal:** Convert 10% of free users to paid tiers

**Tactics:**
- Limit free tier to create "pain points"
- Show upgrade prompts at key moments
- Offer 14-day Pro trial
- Email campaigns highlighting paid features
- In-app notifications when limits are reached

### 2. Usage-Based Upsells

**Goal:** Encourage users to upgrade as usage grows

**Tactics:**
- Alert users when approaching limits
- Offer one-time "top-up" purchases
- Suggest tier upgrade with ROI calculation
- Show usage trends and projections

### 3. Annual Subscriptions

**Goal:** 50% of paid users on annual plans

**Tactics:**
- 20% discount for annual payment
- Offer annual plan during onboarding
- Remind monthly subscribers of savings
- Lock in pricing for annual subscribers

### 4. Enterprise Sales

**Goal:** Land 2-3 enterprise customers in Year 1

**Tactics:**
- Direct sales outreach to large GCs
- Custom demos and pilots
- RFP responses
- Industry conference presence
- Case studies and testimonials

### 5. Add-On Services

**Additional Revenue Streams:**
- **Custom ML Model Training:** $5,000-20,000 one-time
- **Data Migration Services:** $2,000-10,000 one-time
- **Training & Onboarding:** $1,000-5,000 one-time
- **Priority Support:** $500/month add-on
- **Custom Integrations:** $10,000-50,000 one-time

## Payment Processing

### Payment Gateway

**Primary:** Stripe
- Low fees (2.9% + $0.30)
- Excellent developer experience
- Supports subscriptions
- Global payment methods
- Built-in fraud protection

**Backup:** PayPal
- For users without credit cards
- International markets

### Billing Cycle

- **Monthly:** Billed on signup date
- **Annual:** Billed upfront, 20% discount
- **Enterprise:** Custom (monthly, quarterly, or annual)

### Failed Payments

1. Retry automatically after 3 days
2. Email notification to user
3. Downgrade to free tier after 7 days
4. Grace period: 14 days before data deletion
